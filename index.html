Spring Skiing Strategy

01
Ski high in the morning

Head to Mont Fort / Attelas early. Snow is firmest above 2,500m before 11:00.

02
Chase the sun clockwise

South-facing runs mid-morning for perfect corn snow before they soften too much.

03
Afternoons go north

North-facing slopes hold snow later. Savoleyres is ideal after lunch.

04
Layer aggressively

-10°C at 08:30 to +5°C by 14:00 in the sun. Removable layers are essential.
